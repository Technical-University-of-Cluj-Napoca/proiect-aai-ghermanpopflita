ICD-11 Classification of Diseases - Cardiovascular System
Code: BA41.0 - Acute Myocardial Infarction
BA41.01 - Acute ST elevation myocardial infarction (STEMI): An acute coronary syndrome characterized by chest pain, persistent ST-segment elevation on EKG, and subsequent elevation of cardiac biomarkers (troponin).
BA41.02 - Acute non-ST elevation myocardial infarction (NSTEMI): Characterized by ischemic chest discomfort, no ST-segment elevation (but potentially ST-segment depression or T-wave inversion), and significant cardiac troponin release.
Emergency Code: LA22.0 - Cardiac Arrest.
Diagnostic criteria: Chest pain radiating to the left arm or jaw, dyspnea, diaphoresis (sweating), nausea. EKG shows ST elevation >= 1mm in contiguous limb leads, or >= 2mm in precordial leads.
Evidence Level: B
